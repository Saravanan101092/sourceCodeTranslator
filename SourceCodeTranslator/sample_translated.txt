
The regulators of the financial system open the option to extend the closing hours of the operation of the day as an additional measure, after the problems that were recorded on Friday by three financial institutions in connection with the Interbank Electronic Payment System (SPEI).

"The Bank of Mexico (Banxico) and the National Banking and Securities Commission (CNBV) are closely monitoring the operation of all participants with the aim of being able to implement timely additional measures to avoid affecting the service", 

One of the additional measures that could be implemented would be the extension of closing hours for the operation of the day, according to the document.
